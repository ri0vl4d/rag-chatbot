WHY AZURE AI?
Businesses need advanced AI capabilities to enhance decision-making, automate
processes, and drive innovation. Azure AI provides a comprehensive suite of AI tools and
services, enabling organizations to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to achieve
these objectives and stay competitive in the digital age.

PRODUCT OVERVIEW
Azure AI offers a broad range of AI services, including machine learning, cognitive services,
and bot services, designed to help businesses build intelligent applications. With Azure AI,
organizations can develop, deploy, and manage AI solutions at scale, transforming data into
actionable insights and automating complex tasks.
